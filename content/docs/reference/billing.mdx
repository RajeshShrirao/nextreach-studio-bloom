Pulseboard bills on two variables: monthly tracked users and workspace seats. Usage resets on the first day of each billing cycle and historical reports remain available on all paid plans.

This page exists to show range. A good docs showcase should not only include onboarding and feature pages; it should also include the operational pages that buyers always forget until support volume rises.

## Tracked users

Tracked users are counted once per billing cycle. A user is considered "tracked" when they generate at least one event.

<Callout type="info" title="Billing cycle">
  Tracked users are counted once per billing cycle. Usage resets on the 1st of each month.
</Callout>

## Seat rules

- **Owners** - Full workspace access, can change billing
- **Admins** - Manage users and settings
- **Members** - Access dashboards and reports
- **Viewers** - Read-only access

## Plan changes

Owners can change plans at any time from the Billing tab:

- Upgrade takes effect immediately
- Downgrade applies at next billing cycle
- Prorated charges apply for upgrades

## Overage handling

When you exceed your plan limits:

1. Warning notification at 80% usage
2. Soft cap at 100% - features continue
3. Hard cap at 120% - new events blocked

<Callout type="warning" title="Important">
  Trial workspaces pause after 14 days of inactivity. Upgrade to a paid plan to keep your data.
</Callout>

## Key points

- Tracked users are counted once per billing cycle
- Owners can change plans at any time from the Billing tab
- Invoice PDFs and receipts are stored for every billing cycle
- Trial workspaces pause after 14 days of inactivity
